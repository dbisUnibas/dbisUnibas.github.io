Databases and Information Systems Group

Website for the Open Source efforts of the Databases and Information Systems Research Group at University of Basel.

View My GitHub Profile

The main focus of the DBIS group (University of Basel) is the development of novel database concepts and their application to large-scale distributed information systems, in particular to Data Management in the Cloud, Multimedia Information Retrieval, and Service-oriented Architectures & Process-based Systems (Workflows). From a technical perspective, this includes aspects of distribution, reliability, scalability, mobility, heterogeneity, dynamic adaptability, and semantics.

On these pages, we present our open source projects.
